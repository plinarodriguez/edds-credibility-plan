# EDDS Credibility Plan 

This directory defines the credibility plan based on the ASME V&V40 standard for an electronic drug delivery system (EDDS). 

It includes:

- ``Question of Interest``: Describes the specific question, decision, or concern that is being addressed. 
- ``Context of Use``: The specific role and scope of the computational model used to address a question of interest.
- ``Risk Assessment``: reflects potential for model-informed decisions to cause harm or undesirable outcomes. A combination of: 
    - **Decision Consequence**: Severity of harm from an incorrect decision
    - **Model Influence**: Degree to which the model contributes relative to other evidence
- ``Credibility Goals``: Chosen rigor for credibility factors based on the Model Risk, Question of Interest, and COU.
- Additional Files: Includes figures, visualizations, and other relevant files.   



---
ASME V&V 40 Standard: [Assessing Credibility of Computational Modeling through Verification and Validation: Application to Medical Devices](https://www.asme.org/codes-standards/find-codes-standards/assessing-credibility-of-computational-modeling-through-verification-and-validation-application-to-medical-devices)
